Johns Hopkins Taiwanese Student Association

We are the Taiwanese American Student Association at Johns Hopkins University — a community dedicated to celebrating Taiwanese culture, fostering meaningful connections, and creating a welcoming home for students of all backgrounds. Whether you’re here to explore your heritage, meet new friends, or just enjoy great food and fun events, TASA is a place for you. From Night Market and Family Nights to cultural showcases and collaborations across campus, we’re proud to share Taiwan’s vibrant traditions and modern spirit with the Hopkins community.
